Help Center & FAQ

Everything you need to know about how Hostra works.

Getting Started

How do I find a verified house?
Browse listings from your dashboard, every house you see there has already passed inspection. No pending or unverified listings ever show up in your feed.
How do I become a Verifier and start earning?
Sign up as a student, head to Verify Houses from your dashboard, and accept any pending job near you. Take the photos, submit the report, get paid once it's approved.

Trust & Safety

What does the Verified badge actually mean?
It means a real student physically visited that house, took live photos on site, and our AI confirmed those photos match what the landlord claimed, water, tiles, fan, everything.
What happens if a listing turns out to be fake?
It never goes live in the first place. If the photos don't match the claims, the listing stays hidden and the landlord is notified why.
What if the AI gets it wrong?
No AI is perfect, so every rejected report is visible to the landlord with the reason attached, and listings can be resubmitted with clearer photos if something was genuinely missed.

For Landlords

How do I list my property?
Sign up as a landlord, go to Post a New Listing, add your address, price, and claims, and upload reference photos. It stays pending until a verifier confirms it.
What happens after I submit a listing?
A nearby student verifier gets notified, visits in person, and submits a report. If it's approved, your listing goes live with the Verified badge automatically.

Still stuck?

Reach us directly, and we'll usually get back to you within a day.
