We built Hostra because renting in Ogbomoso shouldn't feel like gambling.

Every semester, students move to LAUTECH and step straight into a housing market that runs entirely on trust, and that trust keeps getting broken. Agents post photos of houses that don't exist. Some collect deposits and disappear before move in day. Others show you one house and hand you the keys to a completely different one. By the time you find out, your rent is gone and the semester's already started without you.

The Fix

Hostra runs on one simple idea, nobody should have to trust a stranger's word about a house they haven't seen. So instead of asking you to trust an agent's photos, we send another student, someone who actually lives nearby, to walk the street, take real photos, and check the house against what was promised. Then our AI checks that report line by line, comparing every claim to what's actually in the photos, before a listing ever goes live.

Who We Are

We're LAUTECH students building this for LAUTECH students, because we've heard the stories, and some of us have lived them. Hostra isn't trying to be a big real estate company, it's trying to fix one very specific, very stressful part of being a student here.
